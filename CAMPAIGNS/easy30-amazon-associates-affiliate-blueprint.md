# Easy30 Amazon Associates Affiliate Blueprint

Last updated: 2026-05-29

## Purpose

Plan a careful affiliate setup for ReadEasy30 and MathEasy30.

This is a planning file only. Do not add live affiliate links to either site until the affiliate account, disclosure language, product choices, and page placement are approved.

## Trust rule

Learning content comes first.

Affiliate links should never interrupt lessons, worksheets, timers, or learner flow.

Place affiliate/resource links only in tasteful helper sections, usually near the bottom of printable or resource pages.

## Possible product categories

### ReadEasy30

Potential physical learning aids:

- reading strips
- bookmarks
- finger trackers
- dry erase sleeves
- simple pencils and highlighters
- printable worksheet binders

### MathEasy30

Potential physical learning aids:

- fraction tiles
- base-ten blocks
- number lines
- multiplication cards
- graph paper notebooks
- dry erase boards

## Application description draft

Two free educational resource websites serving families, tutors, adult learners, ESL learners, and students who need calmer reading and math practice. Monetization may include tasteful footer or resource-page affiliate links to relevant physical learning aids such as reading strips, math manipulatives, flashcards, and classroom/home practice tools.

## Compliance notes

Before adding any affiliate link:

- join and be approved by the affiliate program
- read the current program rules
- add a clear affiliate disclosure page
- add a short disclosure near each affiliate link
- use appropriate link attributes where required
- do not claim a product treats dyslexia, ADHD, autism, dyscalculia, or any condition
- do not hide affiliate links
- do not place affiliate links inside learner answers or lesson flow

## Suggested disclosure text

As an affiliate, we may earn from qualifying purchases at no extra cost to you.

## Suggested placement

Best locations:

- bottom of printable worksheet page
- resource page
- parent/helper page
- optional tools section

Avoid:

- top of lesson page
- inside timer
- inside quiz answers
- pop-ups
- forced redirects
