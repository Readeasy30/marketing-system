# Easy30 SEO Keyword Sets

Last updated: 2026-05-29

## Purpose

Use these keyword sets when creating new ReadEasy30 and MathEasy30 pages, lessons, worksheets, meta descriptions, Pinterest descriptions, and short video captions.

Use keywords naturally. Do not stuff them into pages.

## ReadEasy30 keyword targets

Primary long-tail targets:

- free functional literacy worksheets for adults
- remedial reading practice for middle schoolers online
- dyslexia friendly short stories with comprehension questions
- calm reading comprehension activities for ADHD kids
- free daily english reading practice for adult ESL
- how to practice reading signs and forms free

## ReadEasy30 safer wording notes

Use medical or neurodivergent terms carefully.

Safer page wording:

- calm reading practice for learners who need less pressure
- short reading stories with comprehension questions
- functional literacy worksheets for everyday reading
- adult ESL reading practice
- remedial reading practice for middle school skills
- printable reading worksheets for signs, notes, forms, and schedules

Avoid unsupported claims:

- treats dyslexia
- fixes ADHD reading problems
- guaranteed improvement
- official grade-level placement
