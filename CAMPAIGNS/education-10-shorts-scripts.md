# Education 10 Shorts Scripts

Use for YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, TikTok, Facebook Reels, and Pinterest Idea Pins.

Primary site:

- `https://readeasy30.com/`

Primary app link:

- `https://readeasy30.com/app.html`

## Rules

- Keep videos short.
- Speak slowly.
- Use large captions.
- Do not promise guaranteed results.
- Do not shame struggling learners.
- Do not claim medical, diagnosis, or special-education outcomes.
- End with one clear action.

# Video Production Notes

## Format

- Vertical 9:16.
- 15 to 35 seconds.
- Large text captions.
- Calm background.
- No clutter.
- One call-to-action per video.

## Visual style

- Soft blue, teal, cream, and white.
- Large readable text.
- Friendly Bubbles-style icon if available.
- Avoid busy classrooms or stressful scenes.

## Suggested caption text overlays

- Reading practice without shame
- 30 minutes a day
- Start where you are
- Read slowly
- Find proof in the story
- Beginner to Grade 8 readiness
- Adult reading practice
- ESL reading practice
- Free worksheet
