# ESL Learner Practice Campaign

Date: 2026-05-28

## Goal

Help ESL learners use ReadEasy30 and MathEasy30 for calm daily practice.

## Message

Practice English reading and simple math one small step at a time.

## Audience

- ESL learners
- Adult learners
- Parents helping children learn English
- Tutors and community helpers

## Page Ideas

ReadEasy30:

- ESL reading practice
- Simple English reading practice
- Daily reading practice for English learners

MathEasy30:

- Simple math practice for English learners
- Math word practice for ESL learners
- Daily math confidence practice

## Social Ideas

1. Practice one short English reading passage today.
2. Use simple math words slowly.
3. Read the question twice before answering.
4. One calm lesson is better than no lesson.
5. Come back tomorrow and repeat.
