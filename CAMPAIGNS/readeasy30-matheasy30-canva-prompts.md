# ReadEasy30 + MathEasy30 Canva Prompts

Last updated: 2026-05-29

## Purpose

Use these prompts to quickly create social graphics for the first ReadEasy30 + MathEasy30 marketing push.

Keep graphics simple, readable, warm, and uncluttered.

## Brand feel

- calm
- friendly
- helpful
- parent-safe
- adult-learner safe
- no shame
- steady confidence
- simple daily practice

## Design rules

- Use large text.
- Keep words short.
- Avoid clutter.
- Use friendly illustrations or simple icons.
- Make every graphic readable on a phone.
- Do not use harsh school-test visuals.
- Do not use panic, shame, or failure language.
