# ReadEasy30 + MathEasy30 Confidence Campaign

Last updated: 2026-05-28

## Campaign Goal

Introduce ReadEasy30 and MathEasy30 as calm daily practice tools for learners who need confidence, simple steps, and a no-shame way to improve.

## Campaign Name

30 Minutes. One Calm Step.

## Simple Promise

Build reading and math confidence with short daily practice.

## Audience

1. Parents helping children at home.
2. Adults who feel behind in reading or math.
3. ESL learners.
4. Older learners who want patient practice.
5. Tutors, homeschool helpers, churches, libraries, and community groups.

## Core Message

Reading and math feel easier when practice is calm, short, and daily.

ReadEasy30 gives learners a simple reading path.
MathEasy30 gives learners a simple math path.

Both are built to help people practice without pressure.

## Short-Video Scripts

### Script 1: Parent helper tip

Hook: If homework turns into a fight, try this.

Say: Lower the pressure. Pick one short reading task or one math task. Stay calm. Praise the effort, not just the answer.

Close: Small daily practice builds confidence.

### Script 2: Adult learner message

Hook: It is never too late to practice reading or math.

Say: Start with short lessons. Move slowly. You do not have to rush. Skill grows with repetition.

Close: One calm step today is still progress.

### Script 3: Daily routine

Hook: Here is a simple 30-minute learning routine.

Say: Ten minutes reading. Ten minutes math. Ten minutes checking answers and talking through mistakes.

Close: Keep it calm. Come back tomorrow.

### Script 4: Confidence message

Hook: The goal is not perfect. The goal is progress.

Say: ReadEasy30 and MathEasy30 are built around small steps. The learner practices, checks, and keeps moving.

Close: Confidence comes from finishing small wins.

### Script 5: Helper reminder

Hook: A calm helper can change the whole lesson.

Say: Sit beside the learner. Read slowly. Ask one question at a time. Give time to think.

Close: Less pressure. More progress.
