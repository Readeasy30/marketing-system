# ReadEasy30 Week 1 Canva Prompts

Last updated: 2026-05-29

## Purpose

Use these prompts to create simple launch graphics for ReadEasy30 and light sister-site mentions for MathEasy30.

## Design Direction

- Calm
- Warm
- Simple
- High contrast
- Easy to read on mobile
- No clutter
- Friendly but not childish
- Good for parents, adults, ESL learners, helpers, libraries, and community groups

## Suggested Colors

Use soft education colors. Avoid harsh neon.

- warm cream background
- soft blue
- gentle green
- dark navy text
- light yellow accent

## Font Direction

- large readable headline
- simple sans-serif body font
- no thin decorative fonts
- keep text big enough for phones

## Image Rules

- Do not show embarrassed learners.
- Do not show failing grades.
- Do not show medical/school testing scenes.
- Prefer calm reading, notebooks, quiet table, friendly helper, open book, simple progress path.
