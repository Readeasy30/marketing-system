## Day 2: Parent / Helper Post

### Facebook

Helping a struggling reader at home can feel hard.

Try this simple routine:

1. Read one short story together.
2. Ask one question at a time.
3. Let the learner answer slowly.
4. Praise effort, not speed.
5. Stop before frustration takes over.

ReadEasy30 was built for that kind of calm practice.

`https://readeasy30.com/calm-reading-practice-30-minutes-a-day.html`
