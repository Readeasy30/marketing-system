## Video goal

Introduce ReadEasy30 as a calm, no-shame reading practice tool and get the viewer to open the free lesson app.

## Target viewer

- Parent helping a struggling reader
- Adult learner rebuilding confidence
- ESL learner
- Tutor or helper
- Older learner who wants private practice

## Length

30-40 seconds

## Style

- Vertical 9:16
- Calm background
- Large text captions
- Soft blue, teal, cream, and white
- Slow voice
- No busy classroom visuals
- No shame language
- No guaranteed-result claim

# Exact 5-Scene Canva Build

## Scene 1 — Hook

**Time:** 0-4 seconds

**On-screen text:**

Reading practice should not embarrass learners.

**Voiceover:**

Reading practice should not embarrass the learner.

**Visual:**

Plain calm background. Large centered text. Small Bubbles-style icon if available.

**Canva note:**

Use a simple title card. Do not crowd the screen.

---

## Scene 2 — Problem

**Time:** 4-11 seconds

**On-screen text:**

Too fast.
Too hard.
Too public.

**Voiceover:**

Reading can feel stressful when the lesson is too fast, too hard, or too public.

**Visual:**

Three stacked text cards or three soft icons.

**Canva note:**

Animate each phrase in one at a time if easy. Keep it slow.

---

## Scene 3 — Solution

**Time:** 11-23 seconds

**On-screen text:**

Short passage
Read Aloud
Simple questions
Find proof

**Voiceover:**

ReadEasy30 is built for calm practice. Start with a short passage. Use read aloud if needed. Answer simple questions. Then find proof in the story.

**Visual:**

Four simple cards. Use a mock worksheet or browser screenshot style if available.

**Canva note:**

This is the main explanation. Keep the text large.

---

## Scene 4 — Benefit

**Time:** 23-32 seconds

**On-screen text:**

Steady progress.
Not pressure.

**Voiceover:**

The goal is steady progress, not pressure.

**Visual:**

Calm learner image, simple progress bar, or Bubbles-style encouragement card.

**Canva note:**

Make this feel reassuring, not salesy.

---

## Scene 5 — Call to Action

**Time:** 32-40 seconds

**On-screen text:**

Start free at
ReadEasy30.com

**Voiceover:**

Start with the free lesson app at ReadEasy30.com.

Site name, link, and simple button-style graphic: Start Reading Practice.
