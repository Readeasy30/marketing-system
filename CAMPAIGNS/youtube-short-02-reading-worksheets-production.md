## Video goal

Show viewers that ReadEasy30 has free printable reading practice for screen-free lessons, parent help, tutoring, ESL reading, adult reading, and daily practice.

## Target viewer

- Parent or grandparent helping a reader
- Tutor or volunteer
- Adult learner
- ESL learner
- Homeschool helper
- Library or nonprofit literacy helper

## Length

30-40 seconds

## Style

- Vertical 9:16
- Calm worksheet/paper look
- Large readable captions
- Soft blue, teal, cream, and white
- Voice should sound helpful, not salesy
- No shame language
- No guaranteed-result claim

# Exact 5-Scene Canva Build

## Scene 1 — Hook

**Time:** 0-4 seconds

**On-screen text:**

Need reading practice away from the screen?

**Voiceover:**

Need reading practice away from the screen?

**Visual:**

Simple paper worksheet image or clean white card with pencil icon.

**Canva note:**

Use high contrast. Make “away from the screen” easy to read.

---

## Scene 2 — What it includes

**Time:** 4-12 seconds

**On-screen text:**

Short passages
Vocabulary words
Simple questions
Helper tips

**Voiceover:**

ReadEasy30 has free printable reading practice with short passages, vocabulary words, simple questions, and helper tips.

**Visual:**

Four checklist items or worksheet preview cards.

**Canva note:**

Keep each line short. Avoid tiny worksheet screenshots that cannot be read.

---

## Scene 3 — Who it helps

**Time:** 12-22 seconds

**On-screen text:**

Parents
Tutors
Adult learners
ESL learners

**Voiceover:**

It can help parents, tutors, adult learners, ESL learners, and anyone who wants calm reading practice on paper.

**Visual:**

Four simple icons or four cards.

**Canva note:**

Use respectful adult-friendly visuals, not babyish classroom graphics.

---

## Scene 4 — How to use it

**Time:** 22-32 seconds

**On-screen text:**

Print one worksheet.
Read slowly.
Answer from the text.

**Voiceover:**

Print one worksheet. Read slowly. Then answer from the text. No rushing needed.

**Visual:**

Paper card with checkmarks or calm reading table visual.

**Canva note:**

This scene should feel easy and doable.

---

## Scene 5 — Call to Action

**Time:** 32-40 seconds

**On-screen text:**

Open the worksheet hub
ReadEasy30.com

**Voiceover:**

Open the free worksheet hub at ReadEasy30.com.

**Visual:**

Site name and link with a simple button-style graphic: Open Worksheets.

**Canva note:**

Hold the link long enough to read.
