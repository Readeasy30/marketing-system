# CONTACT-FORM SCRIPTS

Short messages to **type or paste by hand** into an organization's official
public contact form. These are not for automation. Rules that always apply:

- **5 submissions per day, maximum.**
- Official public contact page only.
- Personalize the first line (org name, who they serve) — never send identical text.
- Log every submission in the tracker (date, follow-up due).
- If anyone replies "stop"/"unsubscribe"/"not interested" → Opt-out = Yes, done.

Each template is intentionally brief (forms are short, no subject line), honest
about who you are, and gives a one-line way to decline.
