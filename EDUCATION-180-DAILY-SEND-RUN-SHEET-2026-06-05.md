## Contact qualification checklist

Send only when all are true:

- The organization publicly serves learners, families, adults, tutors, or community members.
- The contact method is public and appropriate.
- The message is relevant to the organization.
- The site does not forbid resource submissions.
- The contact is not a private person pulled from a scraped list.
- The organization has not opted out.

Do not send when any are true:

- The contact page says no solicitations and the message would not fit their rules.
- The page is unrelated to learning, families, libraries, adult education, tutoring, or community help.
- The contact appears private or personal.
- The contact came from scraping, guessing, or bulk list harvesting.
- The same organization was already contacted recently.

## What counts as a send

A send counts only when one of these happens:

- A public contact form is submitted.
- A public organization email receives the message.
- A resource-submission page receives the resource.
- A public staff inbox receives the message and the organization is a good fit.

A send does not count when:

- The site blocks contact.
- The organization is not a fit.
- The contact info is private or scraped.
- The message is only drafted but not submitted.
- The contact is a duplicate.
