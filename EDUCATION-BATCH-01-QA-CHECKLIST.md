# Education Batch 01 QA Checklist

Last updated: 2026-06-03

Campaign: **ReadEasy30 + MathEasy30 — 30 Minutes. One Calm Step.**

Purpose: Final check before posting/exporting Batch 01 assets.

## Before export

Check every Canva asset:

- Text is readable on a phone.
- Site names are spelled correctly.
- No guaranteed-result claims.
- No school, medical, diagnostic, or official testing claims.
- No private contact information.
- No payment, ad, tracking, or affiliate language.
- Tone is calm, respectful, and not babyish.

## Link check

Use these links only if they load:

```text
https://readeasy30.com/
https://matheasy30.com/
https://readeasy30.com/app.html
https://matheasy30.com/app.html

https://matheasy30.com/printable-math-worksheets.html
https://readeasy30.com/adult-reading-practice-without-shame.html
```

If a deep link does not load, use the homepage instead.

## First post QA

Before posting Card 1:

- Image attached.
- Reading link included.
- Math link included.
- Copy says free practice.
- Copy does not promise guaranteed results.
- Copy is understandable at a simple reading level.

## First video QA

Before posting Video 01:

- Video plays from start to finish.
- Text is readable on phone.
- Audio, if used, is clear and calm.
- Caption includes ReadEasy30.com and MathEasy30.com.
- No platform-prohibited claims.

## Pinterest QA

Before posting worksheet pins:

- Pin title is simple.
- Pin description is calm and practical.
- Destination URL matches the image topic.
- Reading pin goes to ReadEasy30 worksheet hub.
- Math pin goes to MathEasy30 worksheet hub.

## Tracker QA

After posting/exporting/blocking:

1. Open `CAMPAIGN-PUBLISHING-TRACKER.md`.
2. Add live link, export note, or blocker.
3. Use only these statuses:
   - Published
   - Designed
   - Drafted
   - Blocked
   - Paused
   - Needs edit
4. Do not leave a finished action untracked.

## Stop conditions

Stop and record blocker if:

- Canva export fails.
- Social account is not ready.
- A link is broken.
- A platform blocks the post.
- A post requires payment, tracking, automation, or ad setup.

## Done definition

Batch 01 is safe to post when:

- Card 1 passes QA.
- Video 01 passes QA if used.
- At least one platform is ready.
- Tracker row is ready for live link or blocker.
- No unsafe tools are added.
