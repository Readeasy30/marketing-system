# Education Canva Activation Queue

Last updated: 2026-06-02

Campaign: ReadEasy30 + MathEasy30 — 30 Minutes. One Calm Step.

Use Canva to create simple graphics for manual posting. Keep text large and readable on phones.

## Design rules

- Size for social square: 1080 x 1080.
- Size for short video: 1080 x 1920.
- Use calm colors.
- Use large text.
- Use simple icons or friendly learner/helper images.
- Avoid clutter.
- Avoid shame language.
- Do not use medical, diagnostic, or guaranteed-result claims.

## Brand words

Use:

- calm practice
- one step at a time
- free daily practice
- reading confidence
- math confidence
- no shame
- short lessons
- parent/helper support

Avoid:

- guaranteed improvement
- cure
- diagnosis
- official test
- instant results
