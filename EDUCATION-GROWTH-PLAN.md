# Education Growth Plan

Last updated: 2026-05-28

## Purpose

Use this plan to grow ReadEasy30 and MathEasy30 together as one simple education family.

ReadEasy30 helps learners build reading confidence.
MathEasy30 helps learners build math confidence.

Both products should feel calm, simple, helpful, and safe for adults, parents, helpers, children, ESL learners, and older learners who need practice without shame.

## Connected Repositories

- `Wholelychit/readeasy30.com` — reading product website and lesson app.
- `Wholelychit/matheasy30.com` — math product website and lesson app.
- `Wholelychit/marketing-system` — shared marketing, campaign, SEO, content, and growth control center.

## Shared Positioning

### Simple promise

Thirty minutes a day can build stronger reading and math confidence.

### Plain-language message

Practice a little each day. Move at your own speed. Build skill without pressure.

### Audience

1. Parents helping children.
2. Adults who want stronger reading or math skills.
3. ESL learners.
4. Older learners who want patient practice.
5. Tutors, homeschool helpers, churches, libraries, and community groups.

## Product Roles

| Product | Main Job | Emotional Benefit | Growth Hook |
|---|---|---|---|
| ReadEasy30 | Daily reading practice and future placement path | I can read better without feeling embarrassed | Calm 30-day reading path |
| MathEasy30 | Daily math practice and future skill path | I can solve math one step at a time | Calm 30-day math path |

## Shared Brand Rules

- Use short sentences.
- Use 7th to 9th grade reading level for consumer pages.
- Avoid school-shame language.
- Avoid promising guaranteed results.
- Use words like practice, confidence, calm, step, helper, progress, and daily.
- Explain that adult guidance helps younger learners.
- Keep AI coach language supportive, not medical, diagnostic, or official school testing.

## Traffic Strategy

### Channel 1: Search

Build helpful pages around simple questions:

- How can I help my child read better at home?
- How can adults practice reading online?
- How can I help my child with math facts?
- What is a simple 30-minute reading routine?
- What is a simple 30-minute math routine?

### Channel 2: Short video

Use YouTube Shorts, TikTok, Instagram Reels, and Facebook Reels.

Simple video themes:

- One reading tip in 20 seconds.
- One math confidence tip in 20 seconds.
- Parent helper tip of the day.
- Calm practice reminder.
- Before/after routine example.

### Channel 3: Community sharing

Target groups that already help learners:

- homeschool groups
- tutoring groups
- adult education groups
- ESL groups
- library pages
- parent Facebook groups
- Reddit education/help communities when rules allow

### Channel 4: Printable lead magnets later

Future safe lead magnets:

- 30-day reading tracker
- 30-day math tracker
- parent/helper tip sheet
- daily practice calendar
- confidence certificate

Do not add email automation, payment systems, tracking pixels, or ad scripts until approved.
