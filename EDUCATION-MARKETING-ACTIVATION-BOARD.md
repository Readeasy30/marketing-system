# Education Marketing Activation Board

Last updated: 2026-06-02

Projects: ReadEasy30 and MathEasy30

Campaign: 30 Minutes. One Calm Step.

## Activation decision

This is the active marketing execution board for the education sites.

The locked starting point is already in place:

- ReadEasy30 website code stays in `Wholelychit/readeasy30.com`.
- MathEasy30 website code stays in `Wholelychit/matheasy30.com`.
- Shared marketing, campaign copy, outreach rules, social posts, Canva prompts, and publishing tracking stay in `Wholelychit/marketing-system`.
- No live ads, payment tools, tracking scripts, private keys, public AI tools, or account systems are added without direct approval.

## What activated means now

Activated means the campaign is ready to publish manually from approved copy and visual prompts.

It does not mean live ads, tracking pixels, bulk email, payment setup, scraping, or social-account automation.

## Primary goal

Get the first steady traffic and trust signals for ReadEasy30 and MathEasy30 by publishing helpful, calm education content.

Main visitor actions:

1. Open ReadEasy30 and start reading practice.
2. Open MathEasy30 and start math practice.
3. Print one worksheet or use one helper page.
4. Understand in under 10 seconds that the sites are free, calm, simple, and no-shame.

## Priority audience

1. Parents helping children at home.
2. Adults rebuilding reading or math confidence.
3. ESL learners.
4. Older learners.
5. Tutors, homeschool helpers, churches, libraries, adult education groups, and community nonprofits.

## Posting order by platform

### Facebook

Best first use:

- Parent/helper posts.
- Free worksheet posts.
- Calm daily routine posts.

### LinkedIn

Best first use:

- Adult education message.
- Community nonprofit angle.
- Tutor/helper resource angle.

### Pinterest

Best first use:

- Worksheet pins.
- 30-minute routine pins.
- Parent/helper tip pins.

### YouTube Shorts / TikTok / Instagram Reels / Facebook Reels

Best first use:

- 12 to 20 second simple tip videos.
- One idea per video.
- No clutter.
- Large readable text.

### Reddit

Best first use:

- Helpful answers only.
- No spam.
- No link drop unless rules allow.
- Lead with practical advice, then mention the site only if appropriate.

## Message guardrails

Use:

- free practice
- calm daily practice
- one step at a time
- no shame
- short lessons
- parent/helper support
- adult learners welcome
- ESL learners welcome

Avoid:

- guaranteed results
- cure language
- diagnosis language
- school replacement claims
- “AI tutor” claims unless the feature is live and reviewed
- bulk outreach
- ad income promises

## Done definition for activation

The education marketing system is considered activated when these are complete:

1. Activation board exists.
2. Platform-ready post batch exists.
3. Canva activation queue exists.
4. Publishing tracker includes education campaign rows.
5. Project status points to this activation board.
6. ReadEasy30 and MathEasy30 repo statuses point back to the marketing activation system.

## Current blocker

External publishing still requires access to the user’s actual social accounts or manual posting by the owner.

This repo now contains the prepared campaign assets and execution order, but it cannot publish to outside social platforms by itself.
