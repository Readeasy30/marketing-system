## Simple positioning table

| Audience | Pain point | Message | Site CTA |
|---|---|---|---|
| Parent helping child | Homework becomes stressful | 30 calm minutes can lower the pressure | Start free reading or math practice |
| Adult learner | Embarrassed or overwhelmed | Practice privately, one small step at a time | Start with short lessons |
| ESL learner | Needs simple daily English/math support | Short practice builds confidence | Try free daily practice |
| Tutor/helper | Needs no-fuss practice resource | Free, simple lessons to support daily growth | Share the site with a learner |
| Community group | Needs free learning help | Reading and math practice without shame | Use links in newsletters or resource pages |
