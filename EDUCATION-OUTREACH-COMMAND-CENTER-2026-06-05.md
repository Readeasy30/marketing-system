## Outreach safety rules

1. Use only public contact pages, public emails, or public contact forms.
2. Do not scrape, bulk-send, auto-DM, or bypass website rules.
3. Do not contact incarcerated people directly.
4. For reentry/correctional education, contact staff, libraries, chaplains, reentry nonprofits, probation resource pages, or support organizations only.
5. Stop immediately for any opt-out, no-contact, not-a-fit, or remove request.
6. Follow up only once, after 7–10 days.
7. Do not make claims about guaranteed reading or math improvement.
8. Do not say the sites replace teachers, tutors, schools, or professional evaluation.
9. Keep language simple, kind, and practical.
10. Use both full site addresses when space allows.
