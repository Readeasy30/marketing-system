# EDUCATION PROSPECT QUEUE

The queue is the working list of organizations, sorted so that no one is
contacted the wrong way or twice. The companion spreadsheet (`outreach-tracker.xlsx`)
is the live version of this; this file documents the rules behind it.

## Fields tracked per prospect
- Organization
- Segment (one of the seven)
- Contact Type (one of the five buckets)
- Official contact page (URL) — the page the contact was verified on
- Public email (only if bucket = Direct verified public email)
- State / Region
- Status: Researching → Ready to contact → Initial contact sent → Follow-up sent →
  Replied / In conversation → Opted out → No contact (blocked)
- Date first contacted
- Follow-up due (auto: first contact + 9 days, inside the 7–10 day window)
- Follow-up sent
- Opt-out (Yes/No) — Yes locks the row, permanently
- Source (which directory/page it came from)
- Notes

## How a prospect moves through the queue
1. **Research** — find the org and its official site. Record the public contact
   page. Decide the bucket. Never invent or guess an address.
2. **Ready to contact** — bucket assigned, channel verified on the org's own site.
3. **First contact** — email (if verified public email) or one form submission.
   Log the date. Contact forms are capped at **5/day**.
4. **Follow-up** — exactly one, 7–10 days later, only if no reply and no opt-out.
5. **Close** — Replied, Opted out, or No contact. No further outreach.

## Daily caps
- Contact forms: **5 per day**, hard cap.
- Email: low and personal; respect domain warmup (see standards file).

## What disqualifies a prospect
- No public contact channel on its own site → Blocked/no-contact.
- Any opt-out, now or ever → Opt-out = Yes, permanent.
-  Terms of service that prohibit outreach → Blocked/no-contact.
