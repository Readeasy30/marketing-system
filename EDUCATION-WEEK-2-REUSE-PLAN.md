# Education Week 2 Reuse Plan

Last updated: 2026-06-03

Campaign: ReadEasy30 + MathEasy30 — 30 Minutes. One Calm Step.

Purpose: Reuse the best prepared assets before creating more. This avoids scattered work and keeps the campaign moving.

## Week 2 rule

Do not create a large new batch until at least one prepared Week 1 asset has been manually posted or marked blocked.

Use this order:

1. Post or block Day 1 Canva Card 1.
2. Post or block Video 01.
3. Post or block the reading worksheet pin.
4. Post or block the math worksheet pin.
5. Use one Day 5 outreach script for no more than 5 safe contact forms.
6. Then create only 2 or 3 new assets.

## New assets allowed after reuse

Only create new assets after at least one Week 1 asset has been posted or marked blocked.

Allowed new assets:

1. One testimonial-style graphic without fake testimonials.
2. One parent/tutor checklist graphic.
3. One new short video for worksheet practice.

Do not create 10 new videos at once.

## Tracker update rule

After every action, update:

`CAMPAIGN-PUBLISHING-TRACKER.md`

Use one of these statuses:

- Published
- Blocked
- Designed
- Drafted
- Paused
- Needs edit

## Blocker handling

If an account is not ready:

1. Mark the item `Blocked`.
2. Record the reason.
3. Move to the next prepared asset.
4. Do not redo the same asset.

## Week 2 success definition

Week 2 succeeds if any one of these happens:

- One social post goes live.
- One short video goes live.
- One Pinterest pin goes live.
- Five or fewer safe contact forms are submitted and logged.
- A real blocker is recorded clearly so work can continue elsewhere.
