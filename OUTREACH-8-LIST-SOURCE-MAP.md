# Outreach 8-List Source Map

Last updated: 2026-06-04

Campaign: **ReadEasy30 + MathEasy30**

Main link:

```text
https://readeasy30.com/free-reading-and-math-practice.html
```

Daily goal:

```text
8 lists × 30 qualified contacts = 240 contacts per day
```

## List 1 — Public libraries

Daily goal: 30

Use public library websites, state library directories, branch pages, youth services pages, adult services pages, summer reading pages, program suggestion forms, and outreach request forms.

Preferred contacts: youth services librarian, adult services librarian, outreach coordinator, branch manager, program coordinator.

## List 2 — Adult literacy / ESL / immigrant support

Daily goal: 30

Use adult literacy nonprofit directories, literacy council websites, ESL program pages, immigrant support nonprofits, library adult literacy pages, United Way partner directories, and volunteer literacy tutor programs.

Preferred contacts: program director, literacy coordinator, ESL coordinator, volunteer coordinator.

## List 3 — Preschools / daycares / early learning / Head Start

Daily goal: 30

Use Head Start locator pages, early learning coalitions, daycare/preschool websites, state child care directories, and family resource center pages.

Preferred contacts: director, curriculum coordinator, family services coordinator, parent engagement coordinator.

## List 4 — Adult education / GED / workforce / community colleges

Daily goal: 30

Use community college adult education pages, GED prep programs, workforce development boards, American Job Centers, career centers, and adult basic education directories.

Preferred contacts: adult education director, GED coordinator, workforce program manager, student support staff.

## List 5 — Tutors / homeschool / after-school / parent groups

Daily goal: 30

Use tutoring center websites, homeschool co-op pages, after-school program directories, parent resource centers, and group pages only when outreach is allowed by the rules.

Preferred contacts: program owner, center director, parent group admin, tutor coordinator.

## List 6 — Youth nonprofits / Boys & Girls Clubs / YMCA / community centers

Daily goal: 30

Use Boys & Girls Club pages, YMCA branch pages, community center program pages, youth nonprofit directories, after-school pages, and parks/recreation program pages.

Preferred contacts: youth program director, education coordinator, branch director, community outreach staff.

## List 7 — Churches / ministries / family resource / senior / veteran / disability / food-bank helper groups

Daily goal: 30

Use church family ministry pages, community ministry websites, food pantry/resource pages, family resource center directories, senior center pages, veteran support nonprofits, and disability service organizations.

Preferred contacts: family ministry leader, outreach coordinator, resource navigator, program director, office contact.

## List 8 — Reentry / correctional education / justice-involved adult support

Daily goal: 30

Use reentry nonprofit directories, correctional education pages, jail/prison education staff pages, probation resource pages, chaplaincy program pages, reentry coalition websites, and public law-library/resource pages.

Preferred contacts: reentry program director, education coordinator, library services staff, chaplaincy office, nonprofit staff.

## Safety notes for List 8

Do not contact incarcerated people directly.
Do not contact minors directly.
Do not imply legal, prison, parole, medical, or emergency help.
Offer a free practice resource to staff only.

## Qualification checklist

Every contact must pass:

- Public organization or business page exists.
- Contact route is public.
- Contact role fits one of the 8 lists.
- No duplicate contact is already logged.
- Message is relevant to reading/math support.
- No private data is collected.
- No scraping or bulk blasting is used.
