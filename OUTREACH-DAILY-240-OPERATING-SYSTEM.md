## Hard rule

Do not contact children directly.
Do not contact incarcerated people directly.
Use staff, program, library, chaplain, nonprofit, or public organization contacts only.

## Outreach method

Use one-to-one outreach or contact forms.

Do not scrape emails, buy lists, bulk blast, or auto-send unsolicited email.

Automation may be used only for organizing, deduping, tracking, and preparing drafts.

## Daily workflow

1. Open `OUTREACH-DAILY-240-OPERATING-SYSTEM.md`.
2. Open `OUTREACH-8-LIST-SOURCE-MAP.md`.
3. Build or update today's contact rows in `OUTREACH-DAILY-240-TRACKER.csv`.
4. Use the right template from `OUTREACH-FIRST-EMAIL-BY-LIST.md`.
5. Send manually through a public contact form or one-to-one email.
6. Log every message.
7. Stop on opt-out, no-contact, not-fit, bounce, or duplicate.
8. Follow up once after 7 to 10 days only when appropriate.

## Compliance lock

For email outreach, follow CAN-SPAM basics: truthful header/from information, non-deceptive subject lines, clear organization identity, a way to opt out, and prompt honoring of opt-out requests.

## Today's completion rule

Today is complete when either:

1. 240 qualified contacts are logged as sent, prepared, blocked, or held for review across the 8 lists, or
2. every incomplete list has a clear blocker and next action recorded.

Do not rebuild the outreach plan again tomorrow. Update this file only if the locked target or target lists change.
