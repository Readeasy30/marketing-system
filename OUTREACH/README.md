# Outreach System

Last updated: 2026-06-02

This folder controls practical outreach for ReadEasy30 and MathEasy30.

## Purpose

Use this system to share ReadEasy30 and MathEasy30 with organizations that help learners build reading and math confidence.

Primary audience:

1. Libraries and adult literacy programs.
2. Head Start, early learning, preschool, and parent education programs.
3. Adult education, GED, workforce, and community college programs.
4. Tutors, homeschool groups, and after-school programs.
5. Churches, nonprofits, and community groups.
6. Reentry, workforce, and learner-support programs.

## Core rule

Do not mass-send.

Use official public contact routes only:

- official contact forms
- official program inquiry pages
- verified public organization emails
- public directory pages that point to official contact pages

Do not scrape private personal emails. Do not repeatedly send to organizations that do not reply. Stop immediately on opt-out, no-contact request, or clear mismatch.
