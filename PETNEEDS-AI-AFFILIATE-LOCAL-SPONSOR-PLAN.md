# PetNeeds.ai Affiliate + Local Sponsor Plan

Last updated: 2026-06-02

## Purpose

This file prepares PetNeeds.ai for safe monetization without rushing into bad ads, unsafe health claims, or confusing paid placements.

## Monetization Rule

PetNeeds.ai should **help first, sell later.**

No live affiliate links, paid sponsor blocks, local ads, or display ad scripts should be added until the matching disclosure and review rules are ready.

## Best Early Affiliate Categories

Start with practical supplies, not medical promises.

| Category | Good fit? | Why | Caution |
|---|---:|---|---|
| New puppy supplies | Yes | high intent, simple checklist | avoid health promises |
| New kitten supplies | Yes | high intent, simple checklist | avoid unsafe product claims |
| Aquarium basics | Yes | clear beginner supply need | verify product accuracy |
| Pet ID tags | Yes | safety-related and simple | no exaggerated claims |
| Crates/carriers | Yes | common new pet need | explain size/safety basics |
| Beds/toys | Yes | low-risk content fit | do not overhype |
| Pet food | Maybe later | high demand | careful wording; avoid medical diet advice |
| Supplements | Avoid for now | risky health claims | do not promote without expert review |
| Medication | No | unsafe and regulated | do not promote |
| Emergency treatment products | Avoid | high-risk | point to vet instead |

## Affiliate Disclosure Draft

Use near affiliate links, before or close to the recommendation:

> **Affiliate note:** PetNeeds.ai may earn a commission if you buy through some links. We only use product links to support helpful pet-care content. This information is general and does not replace advice from your veterinarian.

Short version for cards:

> PetNeeds.ai may earn from qualifying product links.

## Product Review Standards

Before recommending a product, score it with this rubric.

| Standard | Question | Pass needed? |
|---|---|---:|
| Safety | Could this product create a pet safety problem if misused? | Yes |
| Fit | Is it right for the page topic and owner need? | Yes |
| Plain explanation | Can we explain why it helps in simple language? | Yes |
| No medical promise | Does the copy avoid diagnosis/treatment claims? | Yes |
| Disclosure | Is the affiliate note close and clear? | Yes |
| Alternatives | Are we recommending a category, not forcing one product? | Preferred |

## Do Not Monetize Yet

Avoid these until expert review or stricter standards exist:

- pet supplements
- medication
- flea/tick treatment recommendations
- prescription food
- urgent illness pages
- emergency pet-care products
- anything that implies diagnosis or treatment

## Safe First Monetization Build Queue

1. Create `Pet Supplies Checklist for New Owners` page.
2. Add affiliate disclosure text to the page template.
3. Create product category sections without live links.
4. Build review scoring sheet before choosing products.
5. Add local sponsor disclosure draft to marketing repo.
6. Build local sponsor inquiry page copy.
7. Only then add live affiliate or sponsor links after approval.
