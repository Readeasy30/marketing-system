# PetNeeds.ai Canva Prompt Sheet

Last updated: 2026-06-02

## Purpose

Use these prompts to create PetNeeds.ai social graphics, Pinterest pins, checklist PDFs, video covers, and simple website visuals in Canva.

## Brand Feel

- warm
- family-friendly
- soft green
- warm cream
- clean white space
- simple icons
- mobile-readable text
- practical, not scary

## Canva Production Rules

- Use large readable headlines.
- Keep text short.
- Avoid scary injury photos.
- Avoid fake medical claims.
- Use PetNeeds.ai footer.
- Make square graphics readable on a phone.
- Make Pinterest pins vertical.
- Make printable PDFs clean and low-ink when possible.
