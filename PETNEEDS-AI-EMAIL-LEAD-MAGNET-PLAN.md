# PetNeeds.ai Email + Lead Magnet Plan

Last updated: 2026-06-02

## Purpose

Create a simple email growth system for PetNeeds.ai using helpful checklists instead of pushy sales.

## First Lead Magnet

**Title:** New Pet Starter Checklist

**Subtitle:** Food, safety, vet visit, supplies, and daily routine basics for new pet owners.

## Who It Is For

- new puppy owners
- new kitten owners
- families bringing home a pet
- first-time pet owners
- people who want a simple list instead of a long article

## Lead Magnet Promise

A simple one-page checklist to help new pet owners prepare the home, gather basic supplies, plan the first vet visit, and build a daily routine.

## Landing Page Draft


### Email 2: Safe Home Setup

Subject: Make your home safer for a new pet

Hi,

Before a new pet arrives, check the space where it will eat, sleep, and play.

Look for cords, medicine, cleaners, trash, small toys, unsafe plants, and food left out.

A safe room is one of the best first gifts you can give a pet.

PetNeeds.ai

### Email 3: First Vet Visit

Subject: Do this early: plan the first vet visit

Hi,

A first vet visit helps you ask the right questions about food, vaccines, parasites, behavior, and daily care.

Bring notes about your pet’s age, food, behavior, and any symptoms you have noticed.

If symptoms are serious, sudden, painful, or getting worse, call your veterinarian right away.

PetNeeds.ai

### Email 4: Family Pet Safety

Subject: A simple family pet rule

Hi,

Teach children to give pets space when they eat, sleep, hide, or seem scared.

Gentle habits protect both the child and the pet.

Start simple. Repeat often.

PetNeeds.ai

### Email 5: What PetNeeds.ai Helps With

PetNeeds.ai is building simple pet-care guides for everyday owners.

You will find checklists, safety tips, beginner guides, and practical questions to ask your veterinarian.

We help you know what to do, what to watch for, and when to call a veterinarian.

PetNeeds.ai

## Newsletter Pattern

Send no more than 1 helpful email per week at the beginning.

Weekly topics:

- one safety tip
- one checklist
- one beginner mistake to avoid
- one seasonal pet-care reminder
- one new guide from PetNeeds.ai

## Email Safety Rules

- no diagnosis
- no medication dosing
- no urgent health advice without a vet/emergency reminder
- no product claims that imply treatment
- unsubscribe option required when using an email platform
- clear affiliate disclosure when product links are included
