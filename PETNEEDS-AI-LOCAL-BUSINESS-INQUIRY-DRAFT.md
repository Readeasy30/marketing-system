## Page Draft

# Local Business Inquiry

PetNeeds.ai is building simple pet-care guides for everyday pet owners.

As the site grows, we may create local resource sections for pet-related businesses.

## Possible Listing Categories

Future local resource categories may include:

- groomers
- trainers
- pet sitters
- dog walkers
- pet stores
- aquarium stores
- shelters and rescues
- pet-friendly local services

## What Makes A Good Fit

A good local resource should be:

- pet-related
- easy for owners to contact
- clear about service area
- family-friendly
- honest about services
- useful to everyday pet owners

## Simple Listing Information

A future listing may include:

- business name
- website
- city or service area
- business category
- short description
- contact page or phone number

## Disclosure Note

If a listing is paid, PetNeeds.ai should clearly label it.

Suggested wording:

> Some local listings may be paid placements. Pet owners should still check services, reviews, availability, pricing, and fit before choosing a provider.
