## Social Post Batch

### Post 1: New Puppy

Bringing home a puppy?

Start with the basics:

- safe sleeping space
- puppy food
- vet visit
- leash and ID tag
- chew toys
- potty routine
- family rules

Small routines make puppy life easier.

PetNeeds.ai

### Post 2: Puppy Safety

Puppies explore with their mouths.

Before a new puppy comes home, check for:

- cords
- medicine
- cleaners
- trash
- small toys
- unsafe plants
- food left out

A safe room is a good first gift.

### Post 3: Turtle Found Outside

Found a turtle outside?

If it is healthy and safe, it may be best to leave it alone.

If it is crossing a road, move it in the same direction it was already going only if you can do so safely.

If it is hurt, call local wildlife help.

### Post 4: Do Not Take Wildlife Home

A turtle found outside may be wild.

Taking it home can hurt the turtle and may be illegal in some places.

When in doubt, contact a wildlife rescue, veterinarian, or local animal authority.

### Post 5: Signs Your Pet Needs A Vet

Call a veterinarian if your pet has:

- trouble breathing
- repeated vomiting
- repeated diarrhea
- heavy bleeding
- severe pain
- toxin exposure
- sudden weakness
- symptoms getting worse

PetNeeds.ai gives general information, not diagnosis.

### Post 6: Goldfish Beginner Tip

Goldfish need more than a bowl.

They need clean water, space, filtration, and steady care.

A simple setup done right is better than a cute setup that makes the fish sick.

### Post 7: Family Pet Rule

Family pet rule:

Children should learn to give pets space when they eat, sleep, hide, or seem scared.

Gentle habits protect both the child and the pet.

### Post 8: New Pet Supplies

New pet checklist:

- food
- water bowl
- safe bed or habitat
- cleaning supplies
- ID tag if needed
- vet/contact info
- safe toys
- daily routine

Start simple. Add more after you know the pet's real needs.

### Post 9: Pet Question Hook

Not sure what your pet needs first?

Start with three questions:

1. Is the pet safe right now?
2. Does it need a veterinarian?
3. What daily routine will keep it healthy?

That is the PetNeeds.ai way.
