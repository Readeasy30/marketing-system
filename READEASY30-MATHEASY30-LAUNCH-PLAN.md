# ReadEasy30 + MathEasy30 Launch Plan

Last updated: 2026-05-28

## Launch Goal

Let thousands of people know about free, calm reading and math practice for adults, students, ESL learners, and learners who need extra support.

## Core Message

Free reading and math practice.

No shame.

No pressure.

Simple daily lessons.

Helpful for adults, students, ESL learners, parents, tutors, and learners who need extra support.

## Sites

### ReadEasy30

Free reading practice toward about an 8th-grade level.

### MathEasy30

Free math practice toward about an 8th-grade level.

## Launch Audience Groups

### Adults

### Special Education Support

## Trust Message

These sites are practice tools.

They do not replace:

- school
- a teacher
- a tutor
- a specialist
- an IEP team
- medical or educational diagnosis

They provide extra practice in a calm format.
