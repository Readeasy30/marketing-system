# ReadEasy30 + MathEasy30 Marketing Starter

Last updated: 2026-05-29

## Purpose

Build early traffic for ReadEasy30 and MathEasy30 with simple, trustworthy, low-pressure marketing.

The two sites should feel like one calm daily-practice family:

- ReadEasy30: reading practice, confidence, comprehension, and no-shame learning.
- MathEasy30: math practice, confidence, skill growth, and no-pressure daily review.

## Main audience

### ReadEasy30

- Parents helping children practice reading
- Adults who want reading practice without shame
- ESL learners
- Older learners who want simple practice
- Tutors, helpers, homeschool families, and literacy volunteers

### MathEasy30

- Parents helping children practice math
- Adults who want basic math review
- Students who need calm repetition
- Homeschool families
- Tutors and helpers

## Main message

Use this simple positioning:

> Thirty minutes a day. Calm practice. Better confidence.

Do not promise grade jumps, guaranteed results, official diagnosis, or certified placement.

## Simple offer

Use this offer language:

- Start with one short lesson.
- Practice slowly.
- Build confidence one day at a time.
- No pressure. No shame. Just steady progress.

Best for quick confidence messages and short tips.

- practice
- confidence
- parent/helper support
- simple daily routine
- steady progress
- no-shame learning
