## Do Not Send

Avoid saying:

- we guarantee signups
- we guarantee player value
- users will win
- gambling is risk-free
- the site is legal everywhere
- we can hide sponsorships
- we can publish without disclosure

Keep outreach professional, simple, and compliance-friendly.
