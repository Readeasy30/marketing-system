## Quick Answer

The most common slot terms are reels, paylines, symbols, wilds, scatters, free spins, bonus rounds, and playthrough rules. These words explain how the game looks, how wins are counted, and what rules may apply to offers or promotions.

## Basic Slot Terms

### Reels

Reels are the vertical columns that spin. A classic slot often has three reels. Many newer slots have five reels or more.

Beginners may want to start with simple 3-reel games because there are fewer symbols and fewer distractions.

### Symbols

Symbols are the pictures on the reels. Classic symbols may include cherries, lemons, bells, bars, and lucky 7s.

When symbols line up in certain ways, the game may show a win or reward in the game rules.

### Paylines

Paylines are the lines that decide which symbol combinations count. Older slots may have one simple line across the middle. Newer slots may have many lines or other ways to count combinations.

Before playing outside sites, check how paylines work so you are not guessing.

### Bet Size

Bet size is the amount used for each spin. On SlotsFreeUSA free demos, this is virtual credit only. There are no deposits, cash prizes, or payouts.

On outside real-money sites, bet size can affect how much money is at risk.

## Bonus Terms

### Wilds

A wild symbol can sometimes stand in for another symbol to help make a match. Wild rules vary by game.

### Scatters

Scatter symbols may trigger special features in some games. Rules vary by game, so read the game information before assuming what a scatter does.

### Free Spins

Free spins are spins that may be offered as part of a bonus or game feature. Outside offers may have limits, expiration dates, or rules about which games qualify.

### Bonus Rounds

A bonus round is an extra game feature. It may include picks, extra spins, or special symbols. The rules depend on the game.

## Offer Terms To Know

### Playthrough

Playthrough, sometimes called rollover, describes activity that may be required before promotional value can be used under the site's rules.

This is one of the most important terms to read before accepting an outside bonus.

### Expiration Date

Some offers expire. Check both the claim deadline and the use deadline.

### Eligible Games

Not every game may count toward a promotion. Some offers only apply to certain games.

### Location Rules

Casino-style offers may depend on where you live. Always check the official terms before signing up anywhere.
