# 30-Day Content Calendar: ReadEasy30 + MathEasy30

Last updated: 2026-06-02

## Purpose

This calendar gives ReadEasy30 and MathEasy30 a simple daily traffic plan.

Use it for Pinterest, Facebook, LinkedIn, YouTube Shorts, Reddit-safe posts where allowed, blog/SEO ideas, and one-to-one outreach.

## Rules

- Keep posts short and helpful.
- Do not shame learners.
- Do not promise guaranteed results.
- Do not mass-post the same message everywhere.
- Adjust wording for each platform.
- Send outreach only through official public contact routes.
- Record where outreach went.
