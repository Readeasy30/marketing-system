# Contact Form Fill-In Batch 01: ReadEasy30 + MathEasy30

Last updated: 2026-06-02

This file is for one-by-one manual contact-form outreach. It keeps outreach organized and avoids guessing.

## Rules

- Send one message at a time.
- Use official public contact routes only.
- Do not mass-send.
- Do not scrape private emails.
- Do not use automated sending.
- Record each sent message in `DAILY-EXECUTION-TRACKER.md`.
- One follow-up only after 7 to 10 days, if appropriate.
- Stop on opt-out, no-contact request, or no response after one follow-up.
