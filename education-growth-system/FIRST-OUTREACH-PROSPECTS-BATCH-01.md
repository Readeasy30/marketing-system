# First Outreach Prospects Batch 01: ReadEasy30 + MathEasy30

Last updated: 2026-06-02

This file records a small, safe first outreach batch for ReadEasy30 and MathEasy30.

## Outreach rule

Do not mass-send.

Use one official public contact route at a time.

Use the message in `OUTREACH-COPY-AND-SCRIPTS.md`.

Record every action in `DAILY-EXECUTION-TRACKER.md`.

Stop on opt-out, no-contact request, or no reply after one follow-up.

## Message to use

Subject:

Free no-login reading and math practice resources

Message:

Hello,

I built two free practice websites that may help patrons or learners who need calm reading and math support:

ReadEasy30.com — simple reading practice and printable reading worksheets
MathEasy30.com — simple math practice and printable math worksheet ideas

The sites are designed to be beginner-friendly, respectful, and easy to use without a login.

They may be helpful for adult learners, ESL learners, parents, tutors, and people who want private practice at home.

Thank you for the work you do for learners in your community.

Gerry Lettrey
Wholelychit
