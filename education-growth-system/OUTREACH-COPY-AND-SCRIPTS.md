# Outreach Copy and Scripts: ReadEasy30 + MathEasy30

Last updated: 2026-06-02

## Outreach safety rules

- Use official public contact forms or public organization contact emails only.
- Do not scrape private emails.
- Do not mass-send.
- Do not send the same message repeatedly.
- One follow-up only after 7 to 10 days.
- Stop on opt-out, no-contact, or no response after one follow-up.
- Keep messages short and useful.
- Do not promise results.
- Do not mention tracking, ads, sales, affiliate links, or payments.

## Main message

ReadEasy30 and MathEasy30 are free, no-login practice websites for people who need calm reading and math support.

They may be useful for adult learners, ESL learners, parents, tutors, libraries, and community education programs.

## What to record after outreach

Record:

- Organization name.
- Contact route used.
- Date sent.
- Resource shared.
- Follow-up date.
- Response status.
- Stop/blocked notes.

## Do not send

Do not send claims like:

- Guaranteed improvement.
- Fix reading problems fast.
- Cure dyslexia.
- Replace teachers or tutors.
- Official school program.
- Proven by research, unless a sourced research page is created later.

Use careful, honest words:

- May help.
- Practice support.
- Free resource.
- Beginner-friendly.
- No-login.
- Calm daily practice.
