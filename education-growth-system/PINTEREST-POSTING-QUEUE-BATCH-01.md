## Rules

- Check each Canva design before posting.
- Make sure the text is readable on a phone.
- Use the most specific landing page available.
- Do not use tracking links yet.
- Do not use affiliate links.
- Do not make guaranteed-result claims.
- Record each posted pin in `DAILY-EXECUTION-TRACKER.md`.
