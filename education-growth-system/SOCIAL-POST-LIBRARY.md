## Reddit-safe post framework

Use only where self-promotion is allowed or after offering real value first.

Title idea:

How do you keep reading or math practice from becoming stressful?

Body:

One thing I have found helpful is keeping the first session very short. Instead of trying to finish a full packet, the learner does one short activity, gets one small win, and stops before frustration builds.

For reading, that might mean one short passage and one question.

For math, it might mean five simple facts or one word problem.

The biggest goal at first is confidence and routine.
