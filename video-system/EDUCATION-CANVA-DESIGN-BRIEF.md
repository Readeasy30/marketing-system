# Education Canva Design Brief

Last updated: 2026-06-02

Use this brief to create ReadEasy30 + MathEasy30 short videos in Canva.

## Design size

Vertical video:

`1080 x 1920`

## Visual style

- Calm colors.
- Large readable text.
- Simple icons.
- No clutter.
- Friendly education feel.
- Easy to read on a phone.

## Brand direction

ReadEasy30:

- calm reading practice
- learners who need confidence
- short passages
- no shame

MathEasy30:

- calm math practice
- one skill at a time
- small wins
- no pressure

Shared message:

> 30 minutes a day. One calm step. More confidence.

## Recommended slide structure

Each short should use 3 to 4 slides.

### Slide 1

Hook.

Example:

`Homework fight? Try this.`

### Slide 2

Simple tip.

Example:

`Pick one small task.`

### Slide 3

Confidence message.

Example:

`Praise effort, not just answers.`

### Slide 4

Call to action.

Example:

`Try ReadEasy30.com and MathEasy30.com.`

## Text rules

- Use short lines.
- Keep text large.
- Avoid tiny captions.
- Use 7th to 9th grade language.
- Avoid medical, diagnostic, or school-official claims.
- Avoid guaranteed result promises.

## Canva asset ideas

- Parent and child reading together.
- Adult learner practicing at a table.
- Simple math worksheet icon.
- Open book icon.
- Calendar with 30 days.
- Calm desk scene.
- Friendly helper sitting beside learner.
